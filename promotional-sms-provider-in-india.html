How Are Transactional and Promotional SMS Different?



Promotional SMS
• Used for sales and marketing.
• Delivered only to non-DND numbers that opted in.
• Allowed only between 9 AM to 9 PM.
• Uses a random 6-character sender ID from operator.

Transactional SMS
• Used for order updates, alerts, account messages.
• Delivered even to DND numbers.
• Can be sent 24/7.
• Uses a proper 6-letter sender ID showing brand name.
