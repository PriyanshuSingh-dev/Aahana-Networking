Difference between Transactional and Promotional SMS

Promotional SMS

  • Marketing and sales communications are sent via Promotional SMS.
  • They are only sent to opt-in and non-DND phone numbers.
  • If they are sent between the hours of 9 a.m. and 9 p.m., Promotional SMS should be delivered.
  • The network operator assigns a random 6-Character Sender ID to promotional communications

Transactional SMS

  • Order and booking alerts, as well as informational messages, are sent to registered clients via Transactional SMS. These messages are not intended to be used for marketing purposes.
  • They are sent to all recipients, regardless of their DND status.
  • They are sent 24 * 7 a day, with no time limit.
  • A 6-Character Sender ID assigned by a corporation can be used to identify transactional. This identifier should match the name of the firm or product.
